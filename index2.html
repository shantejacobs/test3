MISSION STATEMENT

Three Sister Communnity Connection serves the underserved community through advocacy, support and education.


About

Three Sisters Community Connection came into existence from the childhood dream of the 3-R's....Roquel, Rhonda, & Rosalind. As children, they were often involved in serving others in the community. This work ethic was instilled in them by their late grandmother, Lee Quency Martin-Green. Although the 3 sisters had the vision, several close cousins shared this same passion....to give back to others, especially the underserved throug advocacy, support and education.
